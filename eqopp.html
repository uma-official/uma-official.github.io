Equal Opportunities

The Union of Mongolian Architects (UMA) and the Junior Chamber International (JCI Central) are pleased to announce the scientific symposium

“Equal Opportunities: Advancing Universal Design in Mongolia”

  • Date : 18th April 2025
  • Venue : Union of Mongolian Architects, Ulaanbaatar

Purpose of the Symposium

  • Promote awareness and implementation of Universal Design principles in Mongolian architecture.
  • Encourage interdisciplinary dialogue between architects, designers, policymakers, and disability advocates at an international scale.
  • Showcase case studies, technologies, and policies that advance accessibility.

Call for Speakers

  • Submission Deadline: 11th April 2024
  • Submission Form: Register here.

Requirements: An abstract of 300 words max which outlines your paper’s focus, methodology, and relevance to Universal Design and “Equal Opportunities, and Speaker Information of 150 words max which highlights expertise in UD, architecture, or related fields.

To attend the Event

  • Admission: Free but invitation only. Register here.
  • Audience: Architects, designers, policymakers, NGOs, academics, and disability advocates.
  • Language: Presentations in English or Mongolian.
